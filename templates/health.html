🌾 Crop Health Advisor

Identify crop diseases & solutions for better farming!

🍊 Orange

Disease: Citrus Canker (सिट्रस कैंकर)

Symptoms: Yellow lesions on leaves & fruit drop.

Solution: Apply Copper-based fungicides, prune infected branches.

🧅 Onion

Disease: Downy Mildew (डाउन्यी मिलड्यू)

Symptoms: Pale-yellow patches, weak bulbs.

Solution: Use sulfur fungicides, improve air circulation.

🧄 Garlic

Disease: White Rot (व्हाइट रॉट)

Symptoms: Rotting roots, stunted growth.

Solution: Apply soil fungicides, practice crop rotation.

🌾 Wheat

Disease: Rust Disease (रस्ट बीमारी)

Symptoms: Orange spots on leaves, reduced growth.

Solution: Use resistant varieties, apply fungicides like Mancozeb.

🌿 Soybean

Disease: Soybean Rust (सोयाबीन रस्ट)

Symptoms: Small brown spots, lower yield.

Solution: Apply Triazole fungicides, ensure proper drainage.

🌿 Rice

Disease: Blast Disease (ब्लास्ट बीमारी)

Symptoms: Brown spots on leaves, reduced yield.

Solution: Apply Tricyclazole fungicide, maintain proper spacing.

🍅 Tomato

Disease: Leaf Curl Virus (लीफ कर्ल वायरस)

Symptoms: Leaves curl and turn yellow.

Solution: Use virus-free seeds, control whiteflies with neem spray.

🌽 Maize

Disease: Leaf Blight (लीफ ब्लाइट)

Symptoms: Dark brown lesions on leaves.

Solution: Apply fungicides like Azoxystrobin, use disease-resistant varieties.

🍆 Brinjal (Eggplant)

Disease: Wilt Disease (विल्ट बीमारी)

Symptoms: Leaves turn yellow and wilt.

Solution: Improve soil drainage, apply neem oil.

🥔 Potato

Disease: Late Blight (लेट ब्लाइट)

Symptoms: Dark spots on leaves and tubers.

Solution: Apply Copper-based fungicides, rotate crops.

🍃 Coriander (धनिया)

Disease: Powdery Mildew (पाउडरी मिलड्यू)

Symptoms: White powdery spots on leaves.

Solution: Spray Neem oil, use sulfur-based fungicides.

🥜 Chickpea (चना)

Disease: Wilt Disease (विल्ट बीमारी)

Symptoms: Yellowing & wilting of leaves.

Solution: Improve soil drainage, apply organic bio-control agents.
